Background

Fruits forJuice is a company which buys fresh fruits from farmers and sells them to juice markers. The company has been in the fruit industry since 2000. For the past years, Fruit for Juice has been trading with only businesses that are into juice making. Recently, the company has extended its operations by supplying fruits to supermarkets, retailers, restaurants and households that place their orders with FFJ.

How to use the site

This site has two pages. One page is the supplier's side page and another is for Customers.
